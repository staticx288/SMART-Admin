## SMART-Pay Framework

**Logic-Based Payments for Operational Transparency**

**Overview**

SMART-Pay is the intelligent financial engine for enterprise operations---streamlining payroll, project funding, client billing, and internal cost tracking. Designed for real-world business use, it ensures every dollar moves according to clear, traceable, and logic-bound rules.

No gimmicks. No wallets. Just smart, automated finance for teams that get things done.

**1. Pay Interfaces**

- **SMART-Pay Dashboards**: Admin and HR portals for managing compensation, expense approvals, and payment status

- **Employee Pay Profiles**: Each team member is linked to a secure SMART-ID profile with routing info, role, and payment cadence

- **Project Funding Accounts**: Departments or jobs can be assigned budget pools with automatic tracking and disbursement logic

- **Emergency Compensation Flows**: Admin-triggered systems for unplanned recovery work, hazard bonuses, or downtime reimbursement

**2. Payment Logic Architecture**

- Transactions are:

  - Bound to business rules (e.g. timecards, roles, milestones, safety clearances)

  - Logged in SMART-Ledger for audit and compliance

- Supports standard payroll methods (ACH, direct deposit), client payments (invoice-linked), and internal budget reallocation

- Can operate in offline mode and resync when connectivity is restored

**Payroll & Compensation Automation**

- Optional triggers include:

- Invoices are generated based on:

  - Services delivered (e.g. site scan, materials test)

- Funds can flow directly into:

  - Project accounts (for tracking expenses)

  - Department budgets

  - Reserved compensation for key personnel

**Internal Credit & Budget Controls**

- Teams can earn internal credits (or apply budgets) for:

  - Operational efficiency

  - Early project delivery

  - Reducing equipment downtime

- Credits are recorded for internal analytics---not as currency, but as performance metrics or incentives
