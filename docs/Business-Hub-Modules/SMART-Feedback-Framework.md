SMART-Feedback is the enterprise-grade participatory system that captures, processes, and evolves from real-time feedback across operations, clients, technicians, and embedded AI nodes. From traditional form feedback to distributed intelligence, the framework evolves in four stages. Phase 1 is currently active.

This isn't a suggestion box. It's a feedback-based intelligence framework.

## ‚öñÔ∏è Current State: Phase 1 - Enhanced Traditional Feedback

The system provides:

* Familiar interfaces (forms, portals)
* Intelligent routing & categorization
* SLA-based response tracking
* AI-assisted analysis (hidden)
* Resolution logging into SMART-Ledger

Feedback is collected from:

* Clients via SMART-Portals
* Operators via SMART-Dashboards
* Devices via SMART-Devices
* AI modules via SMART-Sync

Categories include:

* Critical Issues
* Compliance & Ethics
* Optimization

Routing is controlled by SmartLogic and all actions are audit-anchored.
